Strength: Deal +1 damage with attacks.
Stubborn: On turn end, gain 1 Strength.
Relenting: On turn end, lose 1 Strength.
Sturdy: Next time you are attacked, reduce the damage by this amount and remove Sturdy.
Blessing: Lose 1 health less from the next attack this turn.

Reduces by one every turn end:
Weak: Deal -50% damage with attacks. Reduces by one on turn end.
Lifesteal: Heal for unblocked damage dealt. Reduces by one on turn end.
Vulnerable: Take +50% damage from every source. Reduces by one on turn end.
Dodge: The next attack is negated. Reduces by one per negation or on turn end.
Burning: Take 3 damage at the end of turn, then reduce Burning by 1.
Plague: On turn end, lose this much health, then reduce Plague by 1.
Slow: Gain one energy next turn, then reduce Slow by 1.
