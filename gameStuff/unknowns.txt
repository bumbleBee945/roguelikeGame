- Room of Echoes -
Strange whispers guide your movement through the dungeon room.
Out of the amalgamation of garbled murmurs, three voices remain distinct.
One urges you to go forward, one urges you to retreat, and one tells you to wait.
[Forward] [Retreat] [Wait]

- Frostbound Hollow -
As you enter the next room, you're suddenly chilled to the bone.
Your body hardens, as you feel the frost inch deeper into your blood.
[Leave] [Wait] [Forward]

- The Gloomveil -
Darkness envelops the room, heightening your other senses.
You hear gears turning, machines clicking, and water rushing.
[Gears] [Machines] [Water]

- Mirror, Mirror -
The room contains nothing, except three mirrors.
The first mirror taunts you, implying you'll never leave the dungeon.
The second mirror comforts you, urging you that it will all be okay.
The third mirror is frightened, telling you to leave you must break one of them.
[First] [Second] [Third]

- Toxic Spores -
As you enter, mushrooms begin growing on the ground at a rapid pace.
They're purple, with yellow and green spots.
[Leave] [Pick] [Wait]

- Rotting Crypt -
You see a tomb in the next room.
[Leave] [Open]>>
You open the tomb, as a skeleton appears with hand outstretched.
[Fight] [Take Hand]>>
The skeleton takes your hand in its own, and places a single pearl with its other.

- Creeping Webs -
The next room has a mess of sticky webs, with freshly-caught prey on them.
Except, this prey isn't bugs and insects, but humans such as yourself.
[Leave] [Slice the Webs]>>
The webs seem to start avoiding your blade, as you're able to move further.
You see the prey on the webs start to be more armored, and stronger.
[Leave] [Slice Further]>>

- The Broken Bell -
In the room is one very large bell.
[Leave] [Ring Bell (10+ Damage)]>> [Break Bell (20+ Damage)]>>>>
Ring: You ring the bell, as it reverberates your entire body it drops its clapper.
Break: You break the bell, shattering it and largely shaking the entire room. You pick up a bell shard.
